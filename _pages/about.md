侯君恺，北京邮电大学未来学院2022级电子信息类（元班）本科生，**教育部全国大学生职业规划大赛北京邮电大学总决赛评审专家组成员，北京市自然科学基金本科生“启研”计划项目负责人，北京邮电大学2025年度“校园十大先锋人物”。**获得第二届全国大学生职业规划大赛全国总决赛金奖暨北京赛区成长赛道总冠军、2023—2024年度国家奖学金、2024中国国际大学生创新大赛北京赛区一等奖（项目负责人）等多项荣誉称号。2024年1月随北京市卓越工程师教育培养计划高校联盟（BUAEEE）代表北京邮电大学赴法国巴黎交流访问，担任**北京市教育委员会、北京高校大学生就业创业指导中心特邀报告嘉宾**。

Hou Junkai, a 2022 undergraduate student majoring in Electronic Information (Yuan Class) at the Future College of Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, is a member of the expert review panel for the National College Students Career Planning Competition (Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications Finals), the project leader for the Beijing Natural Science Foundation Undergraduate "Qiyan" Program, and one of Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications' "Top Ten Campus Pioneers" in 2025. He has received numerous honors, including the Gold Award in the National Finals of the 2nd National College Students Career Planning Competition and the overall champion of the Beijing division's growth track, the 2023-2024 National Scholarship, and the first prize in the Beijing division of the 2024 China International College Students Innovation Competition (project leader). In January 2024, he represented Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications on an exchange visit to Paris, France, as part of the Beijing Excellent Engineer Education and Training Program University Alliance (BUAEEE), and served as a guest speaker for the Beijing Municipal Education Commission and the Beijing Higher Education Student Employment and Entrepreneurship Guidance Center.
